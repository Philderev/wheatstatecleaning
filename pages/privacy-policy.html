Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wheat State Cleaning collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you use our website or services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, phone number, and email address when you fill out a form on our website or contact us by phone.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, provide cleaning services, send appointment confirmations and reminders, and send occasional marketing communications about our services and promotions.

SMS Communications

Wheat State Cleaning may send you text messages including appointment confirmations, service reminders, and promotional offers. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. Message frequency varies, and message and data rates may apply. We do not share your mobile opt-in data or consent with any third parties for their marketing purposes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties for marketing purposes. We may disclose information to trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
